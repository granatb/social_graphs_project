Tenwave, also known as Nostalfagwave, is an aesthetic and artistic movement derived from Vaporwave and influenced by Milleniwave and Acidwave.Its main concept is inspired by the ironic and critical part of Vaporwave. It consists of a critique/parody/satire of cultural products of the 2010s, seeing them as decadent and trashy, remembering with nostalgia and better eyes those of previous eras with the exception of a few
