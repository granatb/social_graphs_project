Animecore is an aesthetic revolving around the visual culture of Japanese anime and manga. Animecore tends to overlap with other aesthetic genres, like Cartooncore, Vaporwave, Webcore, Cyberpunk or Scene, due to Animecore's rise in reaction to "cringe culture" that is increasingly prevalent on the internet.Animecore is not anime itself, but rather the images consumed outside the context of the stories anime is conveying
