Applecore is a semi-ironic name for an aesthetic that centers around apples, and is commonly associated with lust, temptation, coming-of-age, power, and dramatica. Religious applecore is often associated with sin, especially original sin (ie Adam & Eve), and sensuality.Applepunk, a much less common aesthetic focusing on apple cores and decay.In addition, elements of applecore are often borrowed from in many common aesthetics, especially Goth and Lolita
