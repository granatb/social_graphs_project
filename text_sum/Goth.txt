However, goth as a concrete music genre didn't exist until the release of Bauhaus' debut single "Bela Lugosi's Dead." Since the release of that track, many bands have formed to create and expand on the genre, intentionally or not (a lot of the original bands that are considered staples of goth rock actually hate the label, especially musicians such as Andrew Eldritch of Sisters of Mercy).
