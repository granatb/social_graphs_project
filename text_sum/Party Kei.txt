Party Kei is a fashion style developed in the mid-2010s by Canadian YouTuber Jillian Vessey/Pixielocks, modelled after Japanese street fashion. However, the style mutated in the years to follow: Vessey and her fanbase, the 'Confetti Club', now wear louder garments from Western brands such as Lazy Oaf, with party references becoming more overt and rainbows used as a common theme.Since Vessey is neither Japanese nor a fluent speaker of the language, there is controversy over her use of the Japanese 'Kei' suffix, usually attached to styles hailing from the specific Harajuku locale, to describe a fashion now made up of mostly Western brands
