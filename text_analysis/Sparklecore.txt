Sparklecore is an aesthetic based on sparkles, shiny objects, and comfort. It is centered around positivity, joy, and creativity. It involves art, makeup, and DIY. However, those things are not required to fully live this aesthetic. Instead, you must be positive and dependable to others and you must also be compassionate. Sparklecore usually brings comfort and joy to people through fun and happy visuals. Things associated with this aesthetic include glitter, hearts, stars, and light.
