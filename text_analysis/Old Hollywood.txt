Old Hollywood is an aesthetic surrounding the movies, music, and fashion of the 1930's- 1960's. It's meant to draw you into a time before and forget about the current things happening, back into a time where everything felt simpler. It's important to note, however, that this time period was riddled with bigotry, and not actually as idyllic as the media of the time presents.
