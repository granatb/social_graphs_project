Crystalcore is an aesthetic centered around crystal shapes and gemstones of any variety; clear or opaque, multicolored or monochrome, raw or polished. This can range from loose standing crystals, beads, embellishments, jewelry, and much more. Minerals have been found in many locations all over the world and many have associated meanings and alleged special properties. Different cultures and communities vary in beliefs of the special properties crystals may have. Communities and entities that utilize crystals for these meaning range from witches and pagans for use in practice to misguided claims they work better than medicine.
