This article discusses a genre of art, literature, and film from the 18th-20th century. For the contemporary music genre and subculture, see Goth.This page is under constructionGothic is an aesthetic based around gothic architecture, art and literature. Gothic originally started as a type of architecture starting in the 12th century Europe, with significant characteristics being pointy arches and usually applied on flying buttresses. Gothic art would eventually evolve into more than a type of outer design, with the label being applied to artwork, poems, books, fashion and eventually shows and video games.
