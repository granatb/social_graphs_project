View at your own discretion.Cartelcore is an aesthetic originating from the fashion of 1980's drug cartels, notably Pablo Escobar's Medellin Cartel. The aesthetic, geographically, focuses on Miami, Florida, USA, and South America.  It incorporates elements of 1980's fashion and imagery, with big inspirations for Cartelcore including Narcos, Scarface, Miami Vice, Hotline Miami, Grand Theft Auto: Vice City, and Grand Theft Auto: Vice City Stories.
