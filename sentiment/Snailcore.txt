Snailcore is an aesthetic revolving around snails and the things in nature associated with them. It is based off of Fairycore, Goblincore, Cottagecore, and snails.This aesthetic has but is not limited to; Cucumbers, snails, strawberries, rainy weather, mushrooms, and other such nature..Snailcore fashion includes: turtlenecks, messy buns, short skirts, and often are seen with the colors brown, pink, light green, dark green, and other such nature colors. It also includes wearing snail related clothing, such as a shirt with a snail print on it, or snail related jewelry.People who are snailcore often find themselves wanting a pet snail, or having the desire to run out into the rain..
