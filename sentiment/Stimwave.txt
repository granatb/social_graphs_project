NOTE: Neurotypicals should avoid adopting this aesthetic and try Kidcore or maybe Scene instead.Stimwave is a largely visual aesthetic based on colorful fidget toys and sensory gifs often found on Tumblr and Pinterest, for neurodivergents, by neurodivergents. Stim toys were created to help those with autism, ADHD, and other "disorders" under the neurodivergent umbrella, but they began to embrace each other and created surreal gifs and videos involving poking, stretching, squeezing, folding, and cutting objects with often unique viscosity..
