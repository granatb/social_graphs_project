Traditional goth (sometimes shortened to trad goth) came about with the original music genre in the late 70's and early 80's. Trad goths listen to officially classified goth music, such as; goth rock, deathrock, darkwave, coldwave, minimalwave and etherealwave. Trad goth fashion includes backcombed hair, black clothing and dark makeup. . 	 	 	 		 		 		 	 Music is incredibly important to Trad Goths.
