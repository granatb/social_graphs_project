Naturecore is an aesthetic surrounding the theme of animals and other nature related things (ie. plants, forests, flowers, etc)..Naturecore visuals, unsurprisingly, focus a lot on nature and the idyllic and often romanticized version of being out in nature. Of course, that's not to stop Naturecore from going into some of the darker elements of nature (like the Cycle of Life, poisonous plants, and just the general struggle of the animal and plant kingdoms within nature), which can lead to some crossover with Vulture Culture.
