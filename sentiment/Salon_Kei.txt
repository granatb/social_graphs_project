Salon Kei is a style started by men working in Japan's hair and beauty industry. It focusses on modern trends, and was created solely for men..Salon kei is slightly feminine in fashion. It mixes high and low fashion brands, as well as secondhand clothing, with the goal of creating a fashionable look.Visuals include:ShirtsCardigansVintage piecesDesigner / luxury piecesSkinny jeansSuit jackets.Bags - large tote bags are favoredHatsBeaniesScarvesBeltsSunglassesLeather shoes.While natural hair colors and styles are the most popular, any other fashionable hair can be part of this fashion.
