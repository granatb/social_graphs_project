wildness western folklore often includes tales of lone vigilantes travelling the american frontier on horseback more times than not armed with a six shooter revolver or a rifle and prepared to dole out justice visually the western aesthetic shares many similarities to that of country but places an emhasis on imagery of the historical wild west as well as the motifs of vigilantism and adventure
