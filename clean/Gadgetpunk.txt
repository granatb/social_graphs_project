gadgetpunk is an aesthetic relating to the use of gadgets gizmos and inventions that are grounded in pseudoscience and fiction it includes visuals of exposed wires circuit boards glowing parts gears and mechanical assemblies gadgetpunk can overlap with aspects of science academia steampunk and junkpunk gadgetpunk fashion is composed of devices that are often compressed multipurpose or can replicate the functions of large and specialized machines gadgets can either be cobbled together from spare parts and scraps or purposely built in lab settings
