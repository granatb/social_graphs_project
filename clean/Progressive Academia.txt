progressive academia otherwise known as progressive traditionalism is an aesthetic and academic philosophy based upon the style of academic delivery and collegiality at modern urban universities especially those of the commonwealth it is important to note that progressive academia should not considered a pure subset of the larger academia family but more a deconstructed version of it influenced by the commonwealth movements for progressive university education and mid to late architectures an example of a new progressive styled university college is new college toronto progressive especially in the values of diversity and anti conformity progressive academia is a manifestation of the progressive movement in universities and in education at large but it is important to note that progressive academia is still based around the tenets of classic academia while actively rejecting all restrictive views of this particular family of aesthetics making it a syncretic position between
