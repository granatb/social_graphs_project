crust punk also known as crust or stenchcore is a form of music influenced by english punk rock and extreme metal the style which evolved in the early in england often has songs with dark and pessimistic lyrics that linger on political and social ills crust punk also has an associated diy oriented branch of punk garb similar to anarcho punk most clothing is black in color denim jackets and hooded sweatshirts with sewn on patches or vests covered in studs spikes and band patches are characteristic elements of the crust punk style of dress or pants covered in band patches they sometimes wear dreadlocks
