takenoko zoku lit bamboo shoot tribe refers to a style and a type of dance group that was active from the to the in harajuku in a group called kekenoko tried to revive the style teenage girls made up most of the movement and were dressed in a very colorful style takenokozoku fashion is characterized by vivid colors such as red pink and violet clothing tended to be gaudy and loose fitting and was made of cheap materials harem suits which were shapeless jumpsuits were an important part of this fashion
