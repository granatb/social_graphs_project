Started in a Facebook group called [https://www.facebook.com/groups/540157200026292 \ud83c\udf44Mushroomcore \ud83c\udf44], unlike [[Goblincore]], this aesthetic is solely focused on mushrooms and nature. Different types of mushrooms are always used.\n\nMushrooms are universally known and widely used in different cultures, foods, and spiritual practices. \"The Fairy Circle\" is believed to be a portal to the spirit realm as told In many folklore tales. Mushrooms are often associated with the underworld, this is due to the fact that mushrooms decompose dead matter and are linked to the rebirth and recycling of dead matter. \n\nMushrooms can also depict characteristics of good fortune and represent rapid growth. These are all themes that the Mushroomcore aesthetic seeks to portray and admire.\n\n==Visual==\n*Toadstools\n*Shelf mushrooms\n*Fairy rings (Mushroom circles)\n*Forest\n*Moist, muddy soil, mud\n*Dried leaves\n*Stones\n*Insects\n*Frogs, slugs, and snails\n*Gnomes\n*Nature spirits\n*Moss\n*Dead logs\n\n==Fashion==\nMushroomcore fashion would include anything outside friendly and easy to partake mushroom hunting in.
