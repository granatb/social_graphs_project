It is often associated with a feeling of loneliness, but this is not necessarily a bad thing. It is similar to [[Lo-Fi]] aesthetic, in the sense of colour palette and general setting but more strongly associates with [[Lunarpunk]] as it has a strong feel of magic and fictitious happenings.\n\n<br />\n\n==Colour palette==\n[[File:Nightcore palette.png|thumb|A typical Spiritcore palette]]\nThe colour palette is similar to that of [[Vaporwave]], using often muted, purply colours. \n\nAlthough the colours of this aesthetic are not strictly limited to these, these are the primary ones. Often an association to plants and magic are referred which tend to have a colour palette similar to this. Some elements of [[Mushroomcore]] are used\n\n<br />\n\n<br />\n\n==Uses in mainstream media==\nOne prominent example of spiritcore in media would be the Studio Ghibli film Spirited Away. Featuring strong elements of supernatural and primarily set in the night, this is an example of spiritcore.\n\n==Clothing/Fashion==\nAlthough there is no strictly defined fashion for spiritcore, one characteristic is often plain, baggy clothes designed as so the person wearing it can focus on more important things in life. These can include :\n\n*Oversized t-shirt\n*Oversized hoodie\n*Denim, but not tight\n*Beanies\n*Shoes that fit in really\n*Accessories like necklaces and bracelets\n\n==Other visual styles==\nAlthough the primarily described styles are outdoors, quite often this aesthetic can be found indoors as well. The same colour scheme ideas and motifs apply however these are other features.\n\n*Darkly lit rooms\n*Fairy lights\n*Bright moons\n*Features from [[Dark Academia]], like books.\n* Mystical elements for example astrology posters\n\n==Gallery==\n<gallery widths=\"130\">\nFile:Night-3078326 1920.jpg\nFile:Alley-89197 1920.jpg|Although this image does not include purples, it is still considered mystcore\nFile:Buildings-1245953 1920.jpg|A good example of nightcore\nFile:Outdoor Fairy icicle led butterfly curtain light.jpg|Fairy lights\nFile:Mooncore.jpg\nFile:Dark academia aesthetic 7.jpg|Reading books in the night can be an element of spiritcore.\nFile:Spirited Away.gif|Spirited Away, a prominent example of spiritcore, & potentially the source of the main name of the aesthetic\n</gallery>\n[[Category:Visual]]\n[[Category:Needs work]]\n[[Category:Core Suffix]]"}}}]}}}}
