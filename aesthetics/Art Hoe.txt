Created by a Tumblr user known as sensitiveblackperson, the Art Hoe scene was created for POC (people of color) and has been accused of being whitewashed. But the Art Hoe community claims to celebrate diversity. Famous original Art Hoes include actress [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Amandla_Stenberg Amandla Stenberg], stylist [https://twitter.com/rianphin?lang=en Rian Phin], illustrator/musician Jenelle Lewis, and rapper Babeo Baggins.<br />\n\n== Fashion==\nTypical Art Hoe fashion items include the following:\n\n*Fjallraven K\u00e5nken Bags\n*Anything associated with \"Art\" culture or artists works on them\n*Mom jeans\n* Graphic tees\n*Converse sneakers of any colour\n*Art socks\n*Art supplies, paint\n*Overalls/painted overalls\n*Shirts with thin horizontal stripes \n*Striped meadow shirts\n*Any mustard yellow clothing\n*Vans Old Skool, Checkered slip-on, Classics\n*Doc Martens or Mary Janes\n*Colorful hair clips\n\nSome typical hairstyles include:\n\n*Messy buns\n*Bob cut\n*Classic Ponytail\n*Pigtails\n*Microbangs\n\nArt Hoes are drawn to a vintage aesthetic, often shopping at thrift stores, Urban Outfitters, and even some lesser-known fashion brands.
