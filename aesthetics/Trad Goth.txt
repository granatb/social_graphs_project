Trad goths listen to officially classified goth music, such as; goth rock, [[deathrock]], darkwave, coldwave, minimalwave and etherealwave. Trad goth fashion includes backcombed hair, black clothing and dark makeup. \n== Music ==\n[[File:The Cure.jpg|thumb|289x289px]]\nMusic is incredibly important to Trad Goths.
