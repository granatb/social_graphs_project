Knightcore has a large presence on Tumblr. There is also a portion of the aesthetic community that related Knightcore to [[Rangercore]], using the shared themes of combat and nature visuals. Like the related aesthetic [[Princecore]], a section of this aesthetic is centered around gay relationships and the two are often paired together. \n\n== Fashion ==\nDue to their practical purposes, historically accurate and historically-inspired knight attire is generally uncomfortable and regarded as inappropriate for everyday wear and most formal occasions.
