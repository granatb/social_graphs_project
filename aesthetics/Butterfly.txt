Clothing in this aesthetic can vary in focus between a particular species of butterfly, multiple, or fantasy butterflies. The monarch butterfly is especially popular. Though often eccentric and feminine, butterfly and butterfly-inspired aesthetic can be incorporated into many different styles, as well as other aesthetics.
