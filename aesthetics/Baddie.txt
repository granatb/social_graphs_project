It mostly involves showing off expensive items and clothing. Brands like Gucci and Prada are must haves for this aesthetic. Most female rappers and R&B singers have a tendency to fit in this category due to the fact they have the money to spend on high-end brands, such as:\n\n*Cardi B\n*Saweetie\n*Megan Thee Stallion\n*Beyonce\n*Bell Barz\n*Rihanna\n*Nicki Minaj\n*Doja Cat\n\n====Visual====\n*Pampering self (spa days and skin care)\n*Always looking simply divine\n*Taking selfies\n*Playing Deus Ex (2000)\n*Getting beauty sleep\n*Reading magazines\n\n====Fashion ====\n*Silk and satin dresses\n*Faux fur coats\n*Boots\n*Clothing with sparkles, rhinestones, or fur\n*Towel on head (not really fashion, but see gallery for examples)\n*Heels\n\n====Accessories====\n*Jewelry (diamonds, pearls, etc.)\n*Tiaras\n*Cat-eye sunglasses\n*Hoop earrings\n*Chokers\n*Handbags and purses\n\n====Makeup ====\n*Natural glam look\n*Lipstick (usually pink, red, or nude)\n*Black eyeliner\n*Eyeshadow (nude color palettes)\n*Light blush\n*Filled in eyebrows\n\n====Hair====\n*Curls (volume is key)\n*Usually up in buns/ponytails\n\n===Retro Baddie ===\n'''Retro Baddie''' is an aesthetic that mixes items from nostalgia with the Baddie aesthetic. It involves retro music, culture, and old video games from the 80s and 90s.  Unlike other subgenres of the Baddie aesthetic, Retro Baddies tend to have a slightly more fun and laid back air to them, having some potential crossover with Nostalgiacore, Vaporwave, Nintencore, and Synthwave.\n\n===Vintage Baddie===\n'''Vintage Baddie''' is an aesthetic that involves 1980's-1990's vintage items (usually clothing and accessories) with Baddie and some Hip-Hop fashion. Vintage Baddie combines all these elements while retaining the Baddie aura. This aesthetic usually involves:\n\n*Vintage t-shirts\n*Straight leg or baggy fitting vintage jeans/cargo pants\n*Skate shoes\n*Shoes with a flat bottom\n*Chunky sneakers\n*Vintage jewelry (usually gold, silver is seen as well)\n\n=== Y2K Baddie===\n'''Y2K Baddie''' a.k.a Soft Ghetto is and aesthetic that mixes McBling, Barbiecore, Tweencore elements with the Baddie aesthetic. The Y2K Baddie aesthetic mainly focuses on the music, fashion and makeup in that time, with a slight modern twist.
