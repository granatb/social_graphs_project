The term was coined by designer and celebutante '''Steves Peeps''' in 2018. It is named after the mid-2000s fashion craze inspired by tv shows such as ''The OC'' and ''Laguna Beach''. This fashion aesthetic was prevalent in award shows as well as graphic design.
