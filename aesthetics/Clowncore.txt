Content from this aesthetic is usually bright and happy, although some portrayals of clowncore can have horror influences. Other related aesthetics similar to clowncore can also have dark and disturbing content. See: [[Juggalo]] for the goth-inspired subculture. See [[Fanfare]] for the circus/amusement park aesthetic.\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n== Visual ==\nClowncore aesthetic visuals include:\n\n* Clowns\n* Soft toys\n*Rainbows\n* Stickers\n*Circus tents\n* Worm on a string\n* Birthday cake\n* Balloons\n* Clown emoji\n* Gumballs\n* Pom-poms\n\n== Fashion ==\nClowncore fashion often includes:\n\n* Large, colorful shoes\n* Childlike motifs such as rainbows, stars and balloons\n* Brightly dyed hair or wigs\n*Party or jester hats\n* Plastic accessories (similar to [[Decora]])\n* Badges or jewelry with childlike slogans such as \"I jump in puddles\" or \"I got dressed today\"\n* Heavy, clownlike makeup\n* Rainbow stripes\n* Primary colors\n*Overalls or suspenders\n*Circus tights\n\n== Music ==\n\n=== Songs ===\n\n*[https://youtu.be/AeSObvIXJtE Pooter the
