The main theme and philosophy are encouraging curiosity and having a yearning to see it all. The original creator, Tumblr user [https://advenchurre.tumblr.com advenchurre] (now deactivated) has stated \"It's about the idea of navigating the world like you're a protag about to set off on a big cross-country journey. Even if you live in the suburbs\".<ref>https://web.archive.org/web/20190913003833/https://advenchurre.tumblr.com/post/186130379986/people-seemed-to-really-like-the-idea-of, Archived link</ref>\n\n[https://maxisgayandtrans.tumblr.com/post/621964053056733184/advenchurre-people-seemed-to-really-like-the It originated on Tumblr], and was created on July 9th, 2019, but has since then branched out into different social media platforms such as Instagram and TikTok.\n\n==Fashion==\nSince Adventurecore is such a broad aesthetic the standards of fashion are flexible and can be difficult to discern from contemporary fashion. Here it has been sorted into three distinct categories.\n\n=== Stylistic===\nAssociated with daily life, Stylistic Adventurecore is more based on presentation than practical activities.\n\n* Beanies/beatnik caps\n*Cloaks, ponchos, shawls, scarfs\n*Denim/corduroy jackets and pants\n*Docs, sneakers, tennis shoes\n*Long coats; trench coats\n*Satchels\n*Trousers\n*Turtlenecks\n\n=== Practical===\nPractical Adventurecore is more suited for camping, hiking, and outdoor travel, with specialised clothing and equipment to help navigate through the elements.\n\n*Beanies or sun hats\n*Combat or hiking boots\n* Extraneous equipment such as compasses or flashlights\n*Fingerless gloves\n*Hiking backpacks\n*Rain jackets\n*Ski jackets\n\n===Interchangeable===\nBoth practical and stylish!
