Jamcore is also based upon helping the environment, such as reusing jars and other materials, or being self sustainable if possible and growing your own food. Conserving the environment and wildlife is also a big part of Jamcore. It takes heavy inspiration from the [[Honeycore]] aesthetic as well, and are related in many ways.\n\n==Fashion==\nJamcore fashion is split into 2 categories: Aesthetics and functionality.
